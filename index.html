Hello!
I’m Parumita (paa-roo-mita)

I used to be an Economics student with an
acute interest in aesthetically pleasing things. I
always used to love everything that involved
creativity-be it drawing, painting, sculpture or
even makeup & fashion. I also really loved
interacting with all the new tech and gadgets

interactions with a user centered perspective.

I am currently interning at Viraltag and trying to
learn as much as I can through blogs, videos
and anyone I can possibly discuss design with.

In my free time, I love travelling, lifting and eating Pizza.
